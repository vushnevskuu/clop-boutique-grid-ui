Chimala trousers. Founded in 2005 by designer Noriko Machida, Chimala is a New York–based Japanese brand specialising in small-batch denim production. Using pre-war vintage looms, skilled artisans meticulously hand-finish and distress the denim, creating timeless pieces with a sense of nostalgic familiarity.

Delicate holes at the knees, dense fabric, impeccable colour — trousers with a past, made for new stories.
