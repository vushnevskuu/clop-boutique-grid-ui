A vampiric vest made from 100% wool and 100% cotton, adding +100% mystery in any weather.

Structured like a relic rather than a garment, it balances raw knit texture with a tailored inner shell. The silhouette is elongated and sharp, cut close to the body, with an asymmetrical front that feels ceremonial rather than decorative.

Inside, the construction reveals a cruciform seam detail with a small metallic crucifix at the back — subtle, hidden, deliberate. The contrast between coarse outer knit and smooth cotton lining creates depth, weight, and restraint.

This is not a layering piece for warmth, but for presence. It reads dark, quiet, and intentional — something worn by choice, not by trend.


Fit: slim to regular, designed to sit close to the body with an elongated front and a structured back. Measurements may vary slightly due to knit texture and handmade construction.
