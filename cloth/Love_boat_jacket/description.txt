LOVE BOAT is an iconic Japanese fashion brand established in 1994, synonymous with late 1990s and early 2000s Gyaru (gal) street culture. Known for bold, trendy, and independent styles, it famously featured in Shibuya 109. After a hiatus, the brand returned in 2021–2022 with a focus on modern streetwear, including its signature bags, apparel, and nostalgic handheld mirrors.
